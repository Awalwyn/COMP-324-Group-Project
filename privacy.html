PRIVACY POLICY

We recognize that sharing your innermost thoughts can feel deeply personal, and we’re committed to protecting your privacy. Here’s how we handle your data when you “burn” a confession:

  • No Message Storage. Your confession text is never recorded or retained. All inputs are processed client‑side and discarded immediately after the burn animation plays.
  • Anonymous, Aggregate Metrics Only. We maintain a single counter for the total confessions burned and, if you opt in, anonymized demographic buckets (age group, gender, date). No PII or message content is ever stored.
  • Purpose of Data Collection. The counter drives the “Burnt Confessions” display, and aggregate demographics power our non‑identifiable charts.
  • Security & Compliance. We use industry‑standard protections (encryption in transit, secure database rules) and never share or sell any data.

Bottom Line: When you use our Confess page, your words vanish completely from our servers—what remains is only an anonymous tally and, if you choose, a basic demographic snapshot.
